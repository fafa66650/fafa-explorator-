About FAFA Explorator

🇬🇧 English 🟡 Català

FAFA Explorator is a connected treasure hunt in Banyuls-sur-Mer.
🧭 Through 3 themed routes (Maillol, Wine Mystery, Old Port), explore the city solo, with family, or as a team!

💡 Each riddle is geolocated and comes with visual challenges, historical facts or creative missions.

🎯 Earn achievements, unlock bonuses with secret codes, and discover Banyuls like never before!

🔐 FAFA guarantees safety, anti-copy protection and a 100% fun and responsive experience!

FAFA HQ™ – Admin & Control

A secure system allows organizers to generate codes, unlock bonuses, track progress, and schedule mission releases via `.ics` files.

Administration is centralized with auto-sync achievements and reinforced anti-copy protection.

🧠 Used in schools, associations and tourism.
